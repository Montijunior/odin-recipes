Water-fufu and Eru

Water-fufu and Eru is meal which grasp its origin from the south west and north west region of Cameroon. It can however be eaten with garri in the absence of Water-fufu.

Ingredients

  • Dried Eru
  • Water-fufu
  • Red palm oil
  • Cray fish and meat
  • Maggi crevet
  • water leafs
  • Canda and dried fish
  • pepper and salt
  • canwa (optional)

Procedure

  1. Carefully washed and drain the dried eru leafs in fresh and clean water. Wash the eru atleast 3 times to remove all dirts.
  2. Wash and slice the canda and meat.. after washing boiled the meat and canda for atleast 30 minutes for it to be softer
  3. With the meat cooking on the fire, wash and slice the water leafs,remember to wash atleast 3 times.
  4. When the meat is ready and the water leafs sliced, add the sliced water leafs in clean pot and leaf to boiled and let the water dry completely.
  5. Add the eru you wash into the water leafs and stire carefully, while adding your cooked canda and meat with dried fish.
  6. Add red palm oil carefully with the necessary spices pepper, maggi crevet and salt stir all allow to cook for 30 minutes.
  7. adding pepper and canwa is optional.
  8. Your eru is ready now over to Water-fufu,place the Water-fufu in dried pot and stir carefully to remove all possible dots and cook on the fire.
