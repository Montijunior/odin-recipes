Conchaffs and Beans

Conchaffs and Beans is a meal commonly eaten across the country Cameroon it's origin can be traced to the north west and south region

Ingredients

  • Dried corn
  • Red or white beans
  • red palm oil
  • maggi crever
  • dried fish
  • canwa
  • white pepper and fresh pepper
  • corri powder for flavor (optional)

Procedure

  1. Start the preparation by boiling the corn.
  2. Once the corn is ready at the beans and boil together
  3. If the corn and beans are ready remove them and washed
  4. Once ready grind all your spices and keep them aside
  5. Place your red palm oil in the pot until it is hot and add all your spices stir gently till all spices are ready
  6. Add your boiled washed corn and beans and stir till it forms a homogenous mixture.. you can add your dried fish and pepper
  7. allow the meal to cook for atleast 45 minutes.
